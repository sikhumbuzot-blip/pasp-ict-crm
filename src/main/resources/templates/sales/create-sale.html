Direct Sale Tips
When to Use

Use direct sales for immediate transactions, walk-in customers, or sales that don't require lead nurturing.

Customer Selection

Accurate Amount

Direct Sale vs Lead Conversion

  • No lead nurturing required
  • Walk-in or phone sales

  • Tracked through sales pipeline
