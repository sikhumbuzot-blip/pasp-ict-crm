Tips for Creating Leads
Lead Title

Use a clear, descriptive title that summarizes the opportunity. Examples: "Website Redesign for ABC Corp", "Software License Renewal"

Customer Selection

Choose the appropriate customer from the list. If the customer doesn't exist, create a new customer record first.

Estimated Value

Provide your best estimate of the potential deal value. This helps with pipeline forecasting and prioritization.

Assignment

Assign the lead to the most appropriate sales person. You can change the assignment later if needed.
